ShutdownGuard
Sukûrë Stefan Sundin
Iðvertë p|b (pasmane@gmail.com)

Sustabdo Windows iðsijungimà, persikrovimà ar iðsiregistravimà.
Kai ShutdownGuard sustabdo veiksmà, dëkle parodo praneðimà su galimybe tæsti.
Paspauskite vidurinájá pelës klaviðà, jei norite iðkviesti iðjungimo lentelæ.

ShutdownGuard automatiškai ieško atnaujinimu.
Ieškojima išjungti galite ShutdownGuard.ini.

Galite naudoti parametrà '-hide', kad paslëptûmëte dëklo ikonà.

Aplankykite tinklapi http://shutdownguard.googlecode.com/
Atsiliepimus siøskite recover89@gmail.com

Jei ši programa Jums naudinga, paaukokite pinigu.
Apie aukojima galite sužinoti tinklapyje.

Changelog
---------
Customized version:
This customized version is made to more prevent tampering with ShutdownGuard by the user.
Once the tray has been hidden, you must hold the Shift button when attempting shutdown to make the balloon tip show.
You must also hold the Shift button when launching another copy to add the tray icon.
You might want to remove this file so your users won't find that out. :)
Be sure to hide the tray icon on autostart.
Note that Vista can still be easily shutdown, that is beyond my control.
There is nothing to prevent the user from terminating ShutdownGuard from other programs like the Task Manager, you must restrict that on your own.
